# 🚀 AIEAT News Dashboard: Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the AIEAT News Dashboard! Here is how to get your AI-powered curation up and running in minutes.

---

### Step 1: Install the AI Engine
Before launching the app, you need the underlying AI running.
1. Run the script: `Install_AI_Engine.bat`
2. Wait for it to finish downloading the local LLM. (This may take a few minutes as model files are large).

### Step 2: Install the Application
1. Run the `AIEAT_Setup.exe` installer file.
2. Follow the on-screen prompts.
3. Once completed, a shortcut will be placed on your Desktop. Double click it to open!

### Step 3: Set Up Your Profile
1. Click the **👤 Profile** icon on the top right.
2. Give your profile a name (e.g. "My Marketing Team").
3. Set your **Organization Name**. (The AI will use this name to customize translations to sound like they are coming from your team!)
4. Switch to this profile.

### Step 4: Add Your Sources 
1. Go to the **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Manage Sources & Configs**.
3. Import the default list of websites (or upload your own `.csv` file).
4. *Pro-tip:* Select specific keywords like "Artificial Intelligence" to only see news that matters to you.

### Step 5: Start Scraping!
1. On the Dashboard, hit the **Start Scraping** button.
2. The application will scan all websites, score the articles using AI out of 10, and translate them to Thai.
3. Review the top-scoring articles directly in the UI!
